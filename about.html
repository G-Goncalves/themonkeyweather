about

The Monkey Weather or "O Tempo Macaco" in portuguese is a meterologic service system in which the profit of the same goes to Monkey Santuaries and Non-profit Animal Wellfare Organizations.

Created to provide information about the weather whilst also sharing awareness about the dificulties monkeys worldwide are facing everyday.

Did you know?

More than half the world's primates, including apes, lemurs and monkeys, are facing extinction. The main threats are well-known which include habitat loss especially the clearing of tropical forests, and the hunting of primates for food and the illegal wildlife trade.

Source

The Monkey Weather Meteorologic Service was designed and developed by Guilherme R Goncalves as a student project for CESAE DIGITAL - "Centro para o Desenvolvimento de Competências Digitais" in Porto, Portugal.

This was accomplished using IPMA's API.

IPMA - "Instituto Português do Mar e da Atmosfera" is the portuguese statal weather institute which is responsable for providing accurate meteorologic information for Portugal's mainland and islands.

As IPMA's API information is restricted to the portuguese territory, also is The Weather Monkey.
